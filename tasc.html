  • Substitute reagent evaluation: Evaluated multiple antibody vendors and antigen retrieval reagents for performance equivalence under controlled experimental conditions.
  • Cross-institutional coordination: Managed logistics and quality consistency across 100+ members from 5 institutions under high-pressure field conditions.
