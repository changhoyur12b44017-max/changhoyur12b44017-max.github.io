Investigated the role of dopamine receptor D2R in oligodendrocyte differentiation and CNS myelination
  • Designed and submitted an NSTC undergraduate research proposal investigating how dopamine receptor D2R regulates oligodendrocyte precursor cell (OPC) differentiation and myelin formation in the mouse CNS.
  • Performed IHC/IF staining (GFAP, NG2, MBP, PLP markers) to characterize glial cell populations in mouse brain regions (corpus callosum, hippocampus) under inflammatory conditions.
